OWNERSHIP

OWNERSHIP

 The website SAMSUNG is registered on November 29, 1994 according to who.is and owned by SAMSUNG group. SAMSUNG is a global company that produces a various range of products from mobiles to refrigerator.

HISTORY

HISTORY

 SAMSUNG began with Lee Byung-Chul as a small trading company in 1938 in Korea. The first snapshot of the main website of SAMSUNG was taken on November 11, 1996, Whereas,the New Zealand website of SASMUNG www.samsung.com/nz/home, appeared first on October 14, 2005 according to Internet Archive.
